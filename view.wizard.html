Travellers

Enter the names of any travellers who will be sharing costs on your trip.
If you forget someone, you can add them later.

Trip Currency

This is the currency you will be spending while traveling.
You can have more than one Trip Currency.
eg. EUR in Europe, CAD in Canada or USD if you're making purchases in the United States








Traveller's Currency

If/When your fellow travellers pay you back, this is the currency that you pay your expenses with
Eg. If you're a Canadian travelling to the US, your purchases will be in USD, but your credit card bills are in CAD. CAD is your Traveller currency.
